The things that help me greatly when learning code

  • The W3 Schools website is full of helpful information. I can search any topic and usually grasp the concept after trying their demos.
  • I've started running through the btutorials at codecademy.com also. Just hearing a concept explained in different words helps me sometimes.

The things I struggle with most when learning code

  • Remembering the syntax of functions, loops, and methods is challenging for me. I started making some flashcards that I can refer to. I may be on to something!
  • Trying to concentrate to solve the challenges in class. I used to be able to multitask, but the older I get, the more trouble I have tuning out distractions.
